How to play the game

In the Coin Master game, you place a bet between $5 and $10 and choose a color: yellow, red, green, or blue. For example, you might bet $10 on yellow. When you roll the dice, four dice will appear, each showing different colors. If any of the dice match the color you bet on, you win, and your winnings are determined by the number of matching dice. For instance, if three of the dice show yellow, your money will multiply by 3. Additionally, if you bet $5 and your current money is greater than $20, and you lose, your bet of $5 will be multiplied by 4 and that amount will be deducted from your current money or wallet. Enjoy the game and good luck!
